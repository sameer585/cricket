INDIAN CRICKET TEAM

Team-india

The India men's national cricket team, also known as Team India or the Men in Blue, represents India in men's international cricket. It is governed by the Board of Control for Cricket in India, and is a Full Member of the International Cricket Council with Test, One Day International and Twenty20 International status.

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian former international cricketer who was captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. He led India to three ICC trophies, the most for any Indian captain

He is a right handed wicket-keeper batsman; as a batsman, he was most successful in limited overs cricket, scoring over 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals, with a reputation as one of the best finishers in the game.
